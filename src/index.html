+10 Millions collection

Novella provides access to over 10 million collections of e-books, magazines, and other publications. The collection includes various genres, from fiction, non-fiction, business, science, technology, to health and fitness. With advanced search features and recommendations based on reader interests, users can find and enjoy their favorite e-books anytime, anywhere.

Connected With Community

Users of Novella can connect with the community within Novella through discussion forums, reader groups, comments, reviews, and organized events. They can interact with fellow readers and writers, share opinions, book recommendations, and participate in events to expand their network with like-minded individuals.

You Can publish your work!

Once his work has received a lot of interest, you can contact Novella to express your interest in publishing his work in print media. Novella will prepare the process for publishing your work in print media, including the editing process, layout design, printing, and distribution of the work to bookstores and readers.
